JOB TITLE: AI Engineer
COMPANY: TechVentures Egypt (Maadi, Cairo - On-site)

ABOUT THE COMPANY:
TechVentures Egypt is a growing AI startup based in Maadi, Cairo. We specialize in building intelligent automation solutions for the MENA region. Our team works on cutting-edge NLP and Computer Vision projects for leading regional enterprises.

ABOUT THE ROLE:
We are looking for a talented AI Engineer to join our Cairo team. You will work on designing, developing, and deploying machine learning models that solve real business problems. This is a hands-on role perfect for someone passionate about AI who wants to grow with a fast-paced startup.

REQUIREMENTS:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field (AUC, Cairo University, Ain Shams, or equivalent).
- 0-2 years of experience in ML/AI (Fresh graduates with strong projects are welcome).
- Strong proficiency in Python and software engineering best practices.
- Experience with Deep Learning frameworks: PyTorch is preferred, TensorFlow is acceptable.
- Understanding of NLP fundamentals: Transformers, BERT, Text Classification, Named Entity Recognition.
- Experience with building and deploying REST APIs (FastAPI or Flask).
- Familiarity with Docker and containerization concepts.
- Version control with Git/GitHub is mandatory.
- Strong problem-solving skills - competitive programming background is highly valued.

NICE TO HAVE:
- Experience with RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) or LangChain.
- Familiarity with cloud platforms (GCP, AWS, or Azure).
- Experience with vector databases (Pinecone, ChromaDB, FAISS).
- Published papers or Kaggle competition experience.
- Open source contributions.

WHAT WE OFFER:
- Competitive salary package (negotiable based on experience).
- Flexible working hours.
- Health insurance coverage.
- Learning and development budget.
- Opportunity to work on impactful AI projects.
- Modern office in Maadi with free parking.

REQUIREMENTS FOR EGYPTIAN CANDIDATES:
- Must have completed (or currently exempt from) Military Service.
- Must be a Cairo or Giza resident, or willing to relocate.
- Excellent English communication skills (written and spoken).
- Arabic fluency required.
